FAQ

We will communicate on the day of your session. If it is calling for rain we will come up with a plan to determine if we can proceed with your session. If percentages are low, we will most likely do the session, if not, we will reschedule for an alternative date that works for both of us.

Coordinating colours, patterns, and textures work best vs. matching (all white shirts and jeans). Here is a link to my Pinterest board to give you some inspiration. When deciding if outfits work together, I suggest laying everything out on your bed, everything from little hair accessories to shoes. I would be more than happy to give you advice and tips when in the planning stages of your session.

Depending on your visions for a location, together we will come up with the best place to capture you. Examples of types of location: urban bricks, long grass, open field, city. I do have favourite locations in the Cambridge area due to lighting, atmosphere and overall looks/visuals.

Family, maternity and couple sessions will be approximately 30-45 minutes long. Newborn sessions will be around one-hour long as we are on baby’s schedule, this allows for changing + feeding breaks.

To secure your spot, a non-refundable deposit is required.

Life happens, I totally understand. We will work together to come up with an alternative date.

I accept cash, e-transfer, and cheque. Choose whichever payment method is best for you. At this time I do not accept cryptocurrency lol. The remaining full payment (after the deposit) is required on the day of your session.

You will receive your photos via an online gallery that can be downloaded onto a desktop or laptop device. If you do not have access to a computer, you will receive your photos on a USB.

You can click here to contact me and we can chat and arrange your session.

Maternity sessions are booked in your second trimester. The best times for maternity sessions are within the 30-34 week mark, during this time frame, your beautiful belly is showing and we don’t risk getting too close to due date.

We will book an approximate date during your third trimester. When baby is born, just send me a message and we can go over what date/time works best. The session itself will occur within the first couple of weeks, this way baby is still sleepy (easy for posing), and is still in the newborn stage.

You will receive your photos within three weeks of your session date. Within two days of your session, you will receive a few sneak peeks of your photos.

You will receive the designated amount of photos for your session. If there are additional gallery worthy photos, I will give you the option to purchase the additional photos for your gallery. It is an additional $2 per photo. The number of additional photos varies for each session.

I will use my best judgement and professional eye to choose and edit the best photos that capture your essence.
