I'm Kate!

Hi! I’m Kate, I am the face behind Fly Away Photography. I am a lifestyle photographer based in Cambridge and Hamilton Ontario. Ever since I was little I would use my parents’ camera and take pictures on my iPod touch. I wanted to be the photographer, I wanted to be the one to make people laugh and to capture those priceless moments. Launching Fly Away Photography during grade 9 in May 2018 has been one of the most rewarding experiences and I wouldn’t change it for anything. When I am not documenting moments for others, I am playing sports (especially basketball), baking (anything chocolate), being active (paddle boarding is my favourite thing), going on new adventures (I LOVE to travel) and spending time with my favourite people. I am currently studying at McMaster University for Integrated Business and Humanities. In my photos I strive to capture emotion and authentic connections in a natural and fun way. It is my passion to capture these forever memories. I will candidly capture this moment in time for you and yours. Thank you for being here and for supporting my journey, passion, and dream.
